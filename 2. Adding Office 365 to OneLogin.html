Adding Office 365

To add Office 365 to your OneLogin account, start at your dashboard portal and then do the following:

  1. Go to Apps > Add Apps.
  2. Search for Office 365 and select it.

  3. On the Add App page, select WS-Federation under Connectors.

  4. Click Save.
  5. Select the Parameters tab.

  6. Ensure that the Email, ImmutableID, and User Principal Name are mapped to the OneLogin fields Email, AD ID, and Email, respectively. 

    Note. Use userPrincipalName as the OneLogin value for User Principal Name if you are not using Active Directory.

    Groups and Licenses are left blank by default and are configurable based on your organization's specific needs.

  7. Go to the SSO tab and copy the WS-Federation Web SSO Endpoint.

    You will give this to your OneLogin support team when they assist you through the Windows Powershell script configuration process.
